The Pythagorean Theorem

Learning Goal

  1. I can use the pythagorean theorem $a^2+b^2 = c^2$ to find the lengths of the hypotenuse of a right triangle, given the two legs.
  2. I can use the pythagorean theorem $a^2+b^2 = c^2$ to find one of the lengths of a right triangle, given the hypotenuse and one of the legs.
