Task 1

Choose an animal species.

How to choose a species:

  1. Go to the iNaturalist website.
  2. Type the name of a group of animals (for example "Elephants") and select one of the choices that appears.
  3. Click on "Species" and choose a species.

Example: If you search iNaturalist for "Elephants," it will allow you to choose from 4 observed species of elephants. Once you choose your species, click on its image to begin task 2.
