Task 1

Choose an animal species.

How to choose a species:

  1. Go to the iNaturalist website
  2. Type the name of a group of animals, for example "Elephants", and click "View"
  3. Click on "Species" and choose one.

Example: If you search iNaturalist for "Elephants," it will show that there are 4 known species of elephants. Choose one, and click on the image to begin task 2.
