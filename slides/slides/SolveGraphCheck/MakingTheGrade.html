Making the grade

Solving inequalities can help you set goals

Jenna wants to know what grade she needs to get on an assignment in order to raise her grade to at least an 80% for the class. She received 70% on the first assignment, and she let $x$ stand for her grade on the second assignment. She said that her final grade would be the average of the two assignments, which is $\frac{70+x}{2}$, so she wrote the inequality $\frac{70+x}{2} \ge 80$.
