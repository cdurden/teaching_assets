Making the grade

Jenna received a 70% on the first assignment. She wants to know what grade she needs to get on the second assignment in order to raise her grade to at least an 80% for the class. She let $x$ stand for her percentage grade on the second assignment. She said that her final grade would be the average of the two assignments, which is $\frac{70+x}{2}$, so she wrote the inequality $\frac{70+x}{2} \ge 80$.
