Task 3: Communicate with a classmate

For this task, I am asking everyone to make an effort to communicate with a classmate who is in your same algebra class.

How do I contact a classmate?

  1. Post a message on the Schoology discussion board for this section.
  2. Draw, write, or type on the collaborative whiteboard here.

What should I say?

The only requirement regarding content of your message is that it is school-appropriate and it involves an unknown number in some way.
