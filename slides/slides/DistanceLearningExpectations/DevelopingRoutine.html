Developing a daily routine

  1. Each weekday, open the course schedule on Schoology.
  2. Go to the date, and read what tasks you need to complete.
  3. Check the box to show that you have read the information.
  4. Open the assignment, complete the tasks, and submit your work.
  5. If you have questions about the assignments, ask your teacher during office hours or send a Schoology message.
