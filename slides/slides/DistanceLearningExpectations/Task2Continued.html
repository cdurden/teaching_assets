Task 2: Communicate with your teacher

A note about dealing with stress

Success as a student during this distance learning experience will require responsibility and initiative to get your work done. Like a lot of new experiences, there are likely to be parts that are stressful. Please do not hesitate to reach out to my to communicate about how you are doing. Your academic and emotional wellbeing are important to us in the North community, so please let us know if there is something we need to be aware about. Thank you!
