Graph the equation $$x+y = 10$$

Then graph the equation $$y = 4$$

You should have two lines. Where do they cross? $$(\hspace{1em}, \hspace{1em})$$

What is the solution to this system of equations? $$(\hspace{1em}, \hspace{1em})$$

$x = $ weight of the cat
$y = $ weight the rabbit
$x+y = 10$
$y = 4$
A point where all graphs meet is called a solution to a system of equations.
